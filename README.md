# Calmex

CalmEx is a desktop time-tracking application designed to help you stay aware of the time you spend studying or working. 
By helping you track your sessions, CalmEx makes it easier to define your objectives, hit your goals, and maintain a healthy and productive routine.

## Features

* **Time Tracking:** Start, stop, and log the time you spend on specific tasks, studying, or working.
* **Goal Setting:** Define personal objectives and track your progress over time.
* **Productivity Insights:** Stay aware of your time management to optimize your daily routines.
* **Simple Interface:** Clean and easy-to-use user interface (Customized with CSS).
